Bundle strategy

Bundles are upsells, not the only way to buy.

Plugins can be sold individually, grouped into small bundles, or included with business plans. Field tools stay standalone first until real buyer overlap proves a bundle is worth pushing.

View pricingStart order flow
Plugin

Single plugin

Search-friendly individual plugin listings for fast discovery and simple conversion.

  • Best for first downloads
  • Low commitment
  • Clear one-problem positioning
Small bundle

Workflow bundle

Group related plugins around a job-to-be-done, such as career tracking or service business admin.

  • Higher average revenue
  • Still understandable
  • Good annual plan target
Business

Business plan inclusion

Business subscriptions can include plugin access for seats as part of a broader workspace.

  • Per business billing
  • Seat controls
  • Usage monitoring
Field

Standalone field tools

Field apps are intentionally not forced into a bundle at launch.

  • Different niche buyers
  • Different value propositions
  • Bundle later after data
