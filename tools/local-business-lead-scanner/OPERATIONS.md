# Local Business Lead Scanner Operations

## Purpose

This tool scans public local-business website pages for visible lead-readiness signals. It is built for review, cleanup, support, SEO triage, and internal business research. It is not an outreach sender, ranking promise, or private-data enrichment system.

## Production runner requirements

A production or admin runner that injects `fetchPage(url)` must enforce these controls outside the pure scanner module:

- Same-origin fetching only for the submitted site.
- Bounded page cap, using the schema hard cap as the absolute maximum.
- Request timeouts.
- Queue-level rate limits.
- User-visible status for blocked, timed-out, or unreachable pages.
- No credentialed requests.
- No cookies or private session reuse.
- No CAPTCHA solving.
- No admin, checkout, account, cart, login, logout, privacy, or terms scans.
- No automated email, SMS, social, form, or CRM outreach.

## Data handling

Reports should be treated as visible-signal snapshots, not verified business records.

Recommended storage policy:

- Store submitted URL, scan timestamp, score, warnings, and reviewed output only when the user explicitly saves a report.
- Keep raw HTML out of persistent storage unless a private admin evidence bucket is intentionally configured with retention limits.
- Redact or omit raw snippets when exporting to public-facing contexts.
- Keep exported CSV and JSON clearly marked as unverified until reviewed.
- Preserve source page URLs for every discovered contact candidate where future UI supports source-level detail.

## User-facing privacy copy

Suggested page copy:

> Local Business Lead Scanner processes the website URL you submit and scans public pages on that same site for visible contact, local-business, SEO, and conversion-readiness signals. The scanner does not sign in, bypass protected areas, submit forms, send outreach, or verify business ownership. Extracted emails, phone numbers, links, and other details are unverified public-page candidates until you review them.

## Review states

Use explicit review states before exporting or using data in another workflow:

- `unreviewed`: generated by scanner, not checked by a person.
- `accepted`: reviewer confirmed the candidate is useful.
- `rejected`: reviewer removed a false positive or stale candidate.
- `needs_follow_up`: reviewer could not confirm the candidate.

## Release gate

Do not mark the scanner launch-ready until:

- Offline tests pass.
- Example runner completes.
- Public/product page copy links to support and privacy pages.
- Admin runner, if added, enforces same-origin, cap, timeout, and no-credential boundaries.
- Exported reports clearly label extracted details as visible, unverified candidates.
