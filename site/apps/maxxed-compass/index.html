Capabilities

What Compass does

An offline Android compass with true-north guidance, trip tracking, recovery state, night display, local history, and calculated sky tools without an account requirement.

01

Use real sensor signals

Read rotation-vector headings with accelerometer and magnetometer fallback where supported.

02

Switch north modes

Compare magnetic and true north with declination, calibration, and interference guidance.

03

Track trips locally

Pause, resume, stop, segment, save, and recover active trip state on the device.

04

Check the sky

Use calculated object positions, constellation dots, line patterns, red-map mode, and optional camera preview.

Availability

Core navigation and sky features are in release preparation while outdoor heading, notification, recovery, distance, sky comparison, and battery checks continue.

Privacy

Sensor, location, trip, and optional camera-preview data are designed to stay on the device unless the user exports or emails it. Read the detailed privacy policy.

Important limitation

Compass accuracy depends on the phone sensors, calibration, magnetic interference, and field conditions.
