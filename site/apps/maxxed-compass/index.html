Capabilities

What Compass does

An offline compass, true-north guide, trip tracker, and calculated sky scanner with constellation dots and lines, local history, and practical field modes.

01

Reliable heading sources

Uses the rotation vector with accelerometer and magnetometer fallback.

02

Magnetic or true north

Applies geomagnetic declination and reports magnetic interference and calibration state.

03

Trips that recover

Pause, resume, stop, segment, save locally, and recover an active trip after process loss.

04

Constellation Sky Scanner

Search calculated object positions and view supported constellations as distinct star dots with connecting line patterns, a red-map mode, and optional camera preview.

Product truth

Status, privacy, and limits

Clear expectations are part of the product, not an afterthought.

Availability

Constellation rendering is implemented. Compilation, physical heading, notification, process-recovery, outdoor distance, sky comparison, and battery verification remain in progress.

Privacy

The app requests no internet permission. Sensor, location, trip, and camera processing stays on the device. Read the detailed Maxxed Compass privacy policy.

Important limitation

Compass accuracy depends on device sensors, calibration, magnetic conditions, and physical testing.
