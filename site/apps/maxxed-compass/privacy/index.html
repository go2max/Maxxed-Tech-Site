Maxxed Compass Privacy Policy

Maxxed Compass processes device sensors, location, trip history, and optional live camera preview data on the device. The current documented app requests no internet permission and contains no advertising, analytics, account, or cloud-sync SDK. It is designed so navigation and trip data remain local unless the user deliberately exports or emails information.

Data processed

  • Compass, accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer, rotation-vector, calibration, and orientation readings
  • Precise or approximate location for declination, trips, coordinates, distance, and sky-position calculations
  • Trip points, route segments, distance, duration, timestamps, pause/resume state, and locally assigned trip names
  • Live camera frames used only for the optional Sky Scanner preview and not retained as captures by the documented app
  • Settings, units, night-mode preferences, display preferences, calibration state, and active-trip recovery state
  • Support details only if the user chooses to email them, such as device model, Android version, app version, screenshots, or troubleshooting notes

Permissions and purpose

  • Precise or approximate location: Calculate true north, coordinates, sky positions, trip distance, and trip history when the user enables those features.
  • Background location: Continue or restore a trip the user explicitly started while the app is not visible, with Android-controlled permission and notification behavior.
  • Camera: Show an optional live Sky Scanner preview. The documented app does not capture, upload, or retain camera frames for this feature.
  • Notifications: Display trip status with Pause, Resume, Stop, and recovery actions where supported by Android.
  • Foreground location service: Continue an active trip with a visible Android notification.
  • Start after reboot: Restore a previously active, unpaused trip after device restart when Android allows it.

Storage and security

Settings, active trip state, trip points, route segments, and history are stored in Android app-private storage. Camera preview frames are processed ephemerally and are not saved by the documented Sky Scanner flow. Local data is not uploaded to a Maxxed cloud service.

Collection and sharing

The app does not transmit sensor, location, trip, or camera-preview data off the device and does not share it with advertising, analytics, or data-broker services. Data leaves the device only if the user separately exports, screenshots, or emails it outside the app.

Retention

Trip data and settings remain until the user deletes a trip, deletes all local history, clears app data, or uninstalls the app. If the user sends support email, that email is retained only as long as reasonably needed for support, abuse prevention, operational records, or legal obligations.

Deletion

Delete individual trips or all trip history from the app's advanced tools. Android clear-storage and uninstall also remove app-private data, subject to Android backup settings controlled by the device and operating system. Email privacy@techmaxxed.com to request deletion of support records sent to Maxxed Technical Systems.

Third-party services

No third-party advertising, analytics, account, or cloud-sync service is active in the current documented app. Google Play may process installation, security, crash, and device diagnostics under Google's policies.

Children

The app is a navigation utility and is not directed to children. Location, camera, and notification features require device permissions controlled by the device owner.

Contact

Questions may be sent to privacy@techmaxxed.com.
