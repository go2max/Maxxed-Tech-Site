Capabilities

What Fishing does

An offline-first catch journal with guided photos, known-reference length measurement, uncertainty, manual species confirmation, and privacy-minded exports.

01

Reference-based measurement

Measure length from a known reference with draggable correction handles and visible uncertainty.

02

User-confirmed records

Species identification remains an explicit user decision rather than an automated claim.

03

Private local journal

Store catch records on the current device and redact exact coordinates from standard exports.

04

Truthful rule handling

Regulation fixtures can exercise the evaluator but cannot authorize keeping a fish.

Product truth

Status, privacy, and limits

Clear expectations are part of the product, not an afterthought.

Availability

Functional source is present. Build, signing, known-object measurement tests, device evidence, and regulation-source verification remain in progress. Pre-release testing requests are welcome even before a public store launch.

Privacy

Catch records stay private on the device, and default exports redact exact coordinates. Read the detailed Fishing Maxxed privacy policy.

Important limitation

Users must check current official fishing regulations. The app cannot authorize a catch as legal to keep.
