Capabilities

What Fishing does

An offline-first fishing log with catch photos, known-reference length estimates, manual species confirmation, local records, private coordinates, and redacted exports.

01

Measure against a reference

Estimate length from a known object with draggable correction handles and visible uncertainty.

02

Confirm records yourself

Species identification remains a user decision instead of an unsupported automated claim.

03

Keep coordinates private

Store catch records locally and redact exact coordinates from standard exports.

04

Stay honest on rules

Regulation fixtures can support checks, but users still verify current official fishing rules.

Availability

Functional source is present and moving through build, signing, known-object testing, device evidence, and regulation-source verification.

Privacy

Catch records stay private on the device, and standard exports redact exact coordinates by default. Read the detailed privacy policy.

Important limitation

Users must check current official fishing regulations. The app cannot authorize a catch as legal to keep.
