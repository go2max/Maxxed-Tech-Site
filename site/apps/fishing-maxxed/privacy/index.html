Fishing Maxxed Privacy Policy

Fishing Maxxed is an offline-first catch journal. It stores catch records locally, keeps exact coordinates private by default, and redacts exact coordinates from standard exports.

Effective June 23, 2026

Policy scopeThe current documented Android implementation and stated release configuration.
User controlPermissions remain controlled through Android settings and in-app actions.
Contactprivacy@techmaxxed.com

Data processed

  • Catch photographs and known-reference measurement points
  • User-confirmed species, length, optional weight, method, notes, and catch status
  • Exact location stored privately and a broader region used for privacy-safe records
  • Catch dates, local history, filters, and on-device leaderboard results
  • User-directed CSV or sharing exports

Permissions and purpose

CameraCapture a catch photograph and known reference for measurement.
LocationAttach an exact private location and derive a broader region when the user allows it.
Document or sharing accessLet the user explicitly export a privacy-safe record.

Storage and security

Catch photos, exact coordinates, measurements, species confirmation, and notes are stored in app-private local storage.

Maxxed Technical Systems applies reasonable safeguards appropriate to the current architecture, but no device or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Collection and sharing

The app does not provide cloud sync or a global leaderboard. Standard exports redact exact coordinates. A user may still choose to share other record content through Android.

Retention

Catch records remain until the user edits or deletes them, clears app storage, or uninstalls the app.

Deletion

Delete individual catches from the local journal. Clear Android app storage or uninstall to remove all remaining app-private records.

Third-party services

A destination selected through Android's share or document interface controls the exported copy. Bundled regulation fixtures are non-authoritative and do not transmit catch data.

Children

Fishing Maxxed is an outdoor recordkeeping tool and is not directed to children. Device owners control camera and location permission.

Policy changes

Material changes will be published on this page with a revised effective date. A production build will not deliberately enable a new remote data service without updating its disclosures.

Contact

Questions or deletion concerns may be sent to privacy@techmaxxed.com.
