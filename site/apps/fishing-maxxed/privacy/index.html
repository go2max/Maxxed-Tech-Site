Fishing Maxxed Privacy Policy

Fishing Maxxed is an offline-first catch journal. It stores catch records locally, keeps exact coordinates private by default, redacts exact coordinates from standard exports, and does not operate a cloud leaderboard in the current documented release.

Data processed

  • Catch photographs, known-reference measurement points, calibration data, annotated captures, and uncertainty values
  • User-confirmed species, length, optional weight, method, notes, catch status, and manual corrections
  • Exact private location when permission is granted, plus broader region fields used for privacy-safer records and exports
  • Catch dates, timestamps, local history, filters, local rankings, and on-device leaderboard results
  • User-directed CSV, image, or sharing exports with exact-coordinate redaction in standard export flows
  • Support details only if the user chooses to email them, such as app version, device model, screenshots, catch examples, or troubleshooting notes

Permissions and purpose

  • Camera: Capture a catch photograph and known reference for measurement.
  • Location: Attach an exact private location and derive a broader region when the user allows it.
  • Document or sharing access: Let the user explicitly export a privacy-safer record or selected copy.
  • Media or photo access where required by Android: Read or write only the user-selected image or export destination when Android requires a scoped permission or picker flow.

Storage and security

Catch photos, exact coordinates, measurements, species confirmation, notes, status, and local ranking data are stored in Android app-private local storage. The app is designed without developer-operated cloud sync or a global leaderboard for the current documented release.

Collection and sharing

The app does not provide cloud sync or a global leaderboard in the documented release. Standard exports redact exact coordinates by default. A user may still choose to share other record content through Android, and the selected destination controls its own copy.

Retention

Catch records remain until the user edits or deletes them, clears app storage, or uninstalls the app. Exported copies remain wherever the user saved or shared them. Support emails are retained only as long as reasonably needed for support, abuse prevention, operational records, or legal obligations.

Deletion

Delete individual catches from the local journal. Clear Android app storage or uninstall to remove all remaining app-private records. Delete exported copies from the destination chosen by the user. Email privacy@techmaxxed.com to request deletion of support records sent to Maxxed Technical Systems.

Third-party services

A destination selected through Android's share or document interface controls the exported copy. Bundled regulation fixtures are non-authoritative and do not transmit catch data. Google Play may process installation, security, crash, and device diagnostics under Google's policies.

Children

Fishing Maxxed is an outdoor recordkeeping tool and is not directed to children. Device owners control camera and location permissions.

Contact

Questions may be sent to privacy@techmaxxed.com.
