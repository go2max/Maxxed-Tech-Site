Capabilities

What Measure does

A camera measurement app built around known-reference calibration, draggable endpoints, persistent ruler review, common units, confidence notes, saved history, and explicit export.

01

Calibrate from a known reference

Use a real object in the same image instead of pretending the camera can infer exact scale alone.

02

Correct both endpoints

Drag measurement points, review the ruler overlay, and keep the evidence visible before saving.

03

Use practical units

View inches, common fractions, feet, millimeters, centimeters, or meters.

04

Keep or export the result

Save, rename, delete, copy, and explicitly export measurements and annotated captures.

Availability

Camera preview, calibration, correction, history, export, and known-object validation are moving through active development.

Privacy

Measurement captures and history are designed to remain local unless the user explicitly exports, shares, screenshots, or emails them. Read the detailed privacy policy.

Important limitation

Camera-assisted measurements are estimates. The app will not claim survey, lab, or .001-inch accuracy.
