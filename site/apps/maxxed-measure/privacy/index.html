Maxxed Measure Privacy Policy

Maxxed Measure uses the camera and user-entered reference dimensions to create camera-assisted measurement estimates. Measurement images, annotations, calibration data, and history are intended to stay on the device unless the user explicitly exports, shares, screenshots, or emails them.

Data processed

  • Live camera frames and user-captured measurement images
  • Known-reference dimensions, calibration ratios, chosen measurement units, and user-selected precision display
  • User-adjusted calibration points, measurement endpoints, ruler overlays, confidence or uncertainty values, and annotations
  • Measurement results, names, notes, timestamps, saved history records, and annotated captures
  • Export destinations selected through Android's system document picker or share interface
  • Support details only if the user chooses to email them, such as app version, device model, screenshots, measurement examples, or troubleshooting notes

Permissions and purpose

  • Camera: Preview and capture the subject and known-size reference used for a measurement.
  • Document access: Let the user explicitly choose a destination when exporting a result.
  • Media or photo access where required by Android: Read or write only the user-selected image or export destination when Android requires a scoped permission or picker flow.

Storage and security

Captures, annotations, calibration data, settings, and saved measurement history are stored in Android app-private storage unless the user chooses an export destination. The app is designed for local processing and does not maintain a developer-operated cloud copy of measurement records.

Collection and sharing

The app does not automatically upload measurements, images, calibration records, or history. Data leaves app-private storage only when the user copies, shares, exports, screenshots, or emails it. User-selected export destinations and receiving apps control their own copies.

Retention

Saved measurements remain until deleted by the user, app storage is cleared, or the app is uninstalled. Exported copies remain wherever the user saved or shared them. Support emails are retained only as long as reasonably needed for support, abuse prevention, operational records, or legal obligations.

Deletion

Delete individual history items in the app. Clear Android app storage or uninstall to remove remaining app-private records. Delete exported copies from the destination chosen by the user. Email privacy@techmaxxed.com to request deletion of support records sent to Maxxed Technical Systems.

Third-party services

Android's document picker, photo picker, or share interface may pass an exported copy to a destination selected by the user. That destination's privacy practices then apply. Google Play may process installation, security, crash, and device diagnostics under Google's policies.

Children

Maxxed Measure is a general utility and is not directed to children. It is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

Questions may be sent to privacy@techmaxxed.com.
