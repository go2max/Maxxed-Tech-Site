Maxxed Measure Privacy Policy

Maxxed Measure uses the camera and user-entered reference dimensions to create camera-assisted measurement estimates. Measurement images and history are intended to stay on the device unless the user explicitly exports them.

Effective June 23, 2026

Policy scopeThe current documented Android implementation and stated release configuration.
User controlPermissions remain controlled through Android settings and in-app actions.
Contactsupport@techmaxxed.com

This page is the public privacy-policy URL for Maxxed Measure. It must be reviewed against the final signed dependency and permission report before Play Store production release.

Data processed

  • Live camera frames and user-captured measurement images
  • Known-reference dimensions and selected measurement units
  • User-adjusted calibration and measurement endpoints
  • Measurement results, uncertainty, notes, names, and annotated captures
  • Export destinations selected through Android's system interface

Permissions and purpose

CameraPreview and capture the subject and known-size reference used for a measurement.
Document accessLet the user explicitly choose a destination when exporting a result.

Storage and security

Captures, annotations, calibration data, and saved measurement history are stored in app-private storage unless the user chooses an export destination.

Maxxed Technical Systems applies reasonable safeguards appropriate to the current architecture, but no device or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Collection and sharing

The app does not automatically upload measurements. Data leaves app-private storage only when the user copies, shares, or exports it.

Retention

Saved measurements remain until deleted by the user, app storage is cleared, or the app is uninstalled.

Deletion

Delete individual history items in the app. Clear Android app storage or uninstall to remove remaining app-private records.

Third-party services

Android's document picker or share interface may pass an exported copy to a destination selected by the user. That destination's privacy practices then apply.

Children

Maxxed Measure is a general utility and is not designed to collect personal information from children.

Policy changes

Material changes will be published on this page with a revised effective date. A production build will not deliberately enable a new remote data service without updating its disclosures.

Contact

Questions or deletion concerns may be sent to support@techmaxxed.com.
