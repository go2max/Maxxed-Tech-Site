What Rival Rush does

A local Android party arcade with PickOff, RPS Arena, Tap Battle, and Word Rush, built for quick rounds, readable scores, player profiles, and couch competition.

01

PickOff

Enter choices, run rapid eliminations, and keep a short winners history.

02

RPS Arena

Play scored rock-paper-scissors rounds with ties, wins, and an optional one-round bot.

03

Tap Battle

Race for taps against another player or an optional opponent mode.

04

Word Rush

Use mobile A-Z controls to reveal words, track mistakes, and start a fresh round.

Availability

The Android build is in Google Play internal testing while UX, credits, navigation, and Word Rush receive final polish.

Privacy

Player profiles and progress are designed for local play. Store disclosures will match the final advertising and analytics configuration. Read the detailed privacy policy.

Important limitation

Features, art, and balance can change during internal testing.
