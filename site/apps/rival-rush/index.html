Capabilities

What Rival Rush does

A colorful couples party arcade featuring PickOff, RPS Arena, Tap Battle, and Word Rush, with profiles, scores, avatars, and more games planned.

01

PickOff

Enter choices, watch rapid eliminations, and keep a short winners history.

02

RPS Arena

Play scored rock-paper-scissors rounds with ties, wins, and an optional one-round bot.

03

Tap Battle

Race for taps against another player or an optional opponent mode.

04

Word Rush

Use mobile A-Z controls to reveal words, track mistakes, and start a fresh round.

Product truth

Status, privacy, and limits

Clear expectations are part of the product, not an afterthought.

Availability

The Android build is open for tester feedback while UX, credits, and Word Rush receive their final pass. Pre-release testing requests are welcome even before a public store launch.

Privacy

Player profiles and game progress are designed for local play; store disclosures will reflect the final advertising configuration. Read the detailed Rival Rush privacy policy.

Important limitation

Features and visual polish can change during beta testing.
