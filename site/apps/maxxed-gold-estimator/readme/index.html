Maxxed Gold Estimator README

An offline visual material estimator with guided multi-angle capture, reference scale, mask correction, material review, saved batches, and CSV export.

Purpose

Capture six views, calibrate against a known reference, review the subject mask and visible material assignments, then produce conservative ranges and confidence notes entirely on the device.

Current Status

Beta testers needed. The requested workflow is implemented. Automated checks, signed builds, physical fixture testing, and final disclosures remain in progress.

Pre-release Testing

Even if Maxxed Gold Estimator is still in development, testers can request access. A clear request helps us prioritize the next launch pass, prepare a tester build quickly when feasible, and collect real-device feedback before public release.

Core Workflow

  1. Guided six-angle captureCollect top, front, right, back, left, and low-oblique views with coverage and image-quality checks.
  2. User-reviewed analysisAdjust mask sensitivity and explicitly confirm or change every visible-material cluster.
  3. Coherent estimate rangesReview visible share, density, volume, weight, and confidence ranges with gold listed first.
  4. Compare and exportSave local batches, compare two results, copy a summary, or choose a CSV destination.

Privacy

Captures remain in app-private storage and leave only when the user chooses an export destination. The full app-specific policy is available at Maxxed Gold Estimator Privacy Policy.

Known Limitation

Color and surface appearance cannot identify chemistry. Results are not an assay or a valuation.
