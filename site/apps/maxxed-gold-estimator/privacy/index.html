Maxxed Gold Estimator Privacy Policy

Maxxed Gold Estimator performs guided image capture and conservative visual material estimation on the device. It has no account, advertising SDK, analytics SDK, cloud sync, or network permission.

Effective June 23, 2026

Policy scopeThe current documented Android implementation and stated release configuration.
User controlPermissions remain controlled through Android settings and in-app actions.
Contactsupport@techmaxxed.com

This page is the public privacy-policy URL for Maxxed Gold Estimator. It must be reviewed against the final signed dependency and permission report before Play Store production release.

Data processed

  • Six guided sample photographs
  • Device orientation used to confirm view coverage
  • Known-reference dimensions and optional measured total batch weight
  • Subject masks, visible color clusters, user material assignments, and confidence ranges
  • Saved batch names, comparisons, notes, and user-directed CSV exports

Permissions and purpose

CameraCapture the six sample views used for on-device visual analysis.
Device motion sensorsConfirm adequate separation between requested capture angles.
Document accessLet the user explicitly select a destination for CSV export.

Storage and security

Captures and saved batches remain in app-private storage and are excluded from backup where supported by the final Android configuration.

Maxxed Technical Systems applies reasonable safeguards appropriate to the current architecture, but no device or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Collection and sharing

The app does not transmit sample photographs, estimates, or batch data. A CSV leaves app-private storage only after the user chooses a destination.

Retention

Captures and batch results remain until the user deletes the batch, clears app data, or uninstalls the app.

Deletion

Delete saved batches inside the app. Android clear-storage and uninstall remove remaining app-private data.

Third-party services

A user-selected document or sharing destination controls any exported copy. The current app does not use a remote analysis provider.

Children

The app is a field estimation tool and is not directed to children.

Policy changes

Material changes will be published on this page with a revised effective date. A production build will not deliberately enable a new remote data service without updating its disclosures.

Contact

Questions or deletion concerns may be sent to support@techmaxxed.com.
