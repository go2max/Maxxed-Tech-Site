Maxxed Gold Estimator Privacy Policy

Maxxed Gold Estimator performs guided image capture and conservative visual material estimation on the device. The current documented app has no account, advertising SDK, analytics SDK, cloud sync, or network permission. It provides visual estimates only and does not perform assay, valuation, or chemical verification.

Data processed

  • Guided sample photographs, including multiple viewing angles and quality checks
  • Device orientation or motion data used to confirm view coverage and separation between requested angles
  • Known-reference dimensions, optional measured total batch weight, and user-entered context
  • Subject masks, visible color clusters, user material assignments, confidence ranges, and conservative estimate outputs
  • Saved batch names, comparisons, notes, timestamps, and user-directed CSV exports
  • Support details only if the user chooses to email them, such as app version, device model, screenshots, fixture examples, or troubleshooting notes

Permissions and purpose

  • Camera: Capture the sample views used for on-device visual analysis.
  • Device motion sensors: Confirm adequate separation between requested capture angles and improve guided capture quality.
  • Document access: Let the user explicitly select a destination for CSV export.
  • Media or photo access where required by Android: Read or write only the user-selected image or export destination when Android requires a scoped permission or picker flow.

Storage and security

Captures, masks, user corrections, saved batches, notes, and estimates remain in Android app-private storage and are excluded from backup where supported by the final Android configuration. The app is designed for local processing and does not maintain a developer-operated cloud copy of sample photographs or estimates.

Collection and sharing

The app does not transmit sample photographs, material assignments, estimates, or batch data to Maxxed Technical Systems, advertising networks, analytics providers, assay providers, or data brokers. A CSV, screenshot, or shared summary leaves app-private storage only after the user chooses a destination.

Retention

Captures and batch results remain until the user deletes the batch, clears app data, or uninstalls the app. Exported copies remain wherever the user saved or shared them. Support emails are retained only as long as reasonably needed for support, abuse prevention, operational records, or legal obligations.

Deletion

Delete saved batches inside the app. Android clear-storage and uninstall remove remaining app-private data. Delete exported copies from the destination chosen by the user. Email privacy@techmaxxed.com to request deletion of support records sent to Maxxed Technical Systems.

Third-party services

A user-selected document or sharing destination controls any exported copy. The current documented app does not use a remote analysis, assay, valuation, advertising, analytics, or cloud-sync provider. Google Play may process installation, security, crash, and device diagnostics under Google's policies.

Children

The app is a field estimation tool and is not directed to children. It is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

Questions may be sent to privacy@techmaxxed.com.
