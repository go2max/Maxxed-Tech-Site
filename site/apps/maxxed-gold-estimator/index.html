Capabilities

What Gold Estimator does

An offline visual material estimator with guided multi-angle capture, reference scale, mask correction, material review, saved batches, and CSV export.

01

Guided six-angle capture

Collect top, front, right, back, left, and low-oblique views with coverage and image-quality checks.

02

User-reviewed analysis

Adjust mask sensitivity and explicitly confirm or change every visible-material cluster.

03

Coherent estimate ranges

Review visible share, density, volume, weight, and confidence ranges with gold listed first.

04

Compare and export

Save local batches, compare two results, copy a summary, or choose a CSV destination.

Product truth

Status, privacy, and limits

Clear expectations are part of the product, not an afterthought.

Availability

The requested workflow is implemented. Automated checks, signed builds, physical fixture testing, and final disclosures remain in progress. Pre-release testing requests are welcome even before a public store launch.

Privacy

Captures remain in app-private storage and leave only when the user chooses an export destination. Read the detailed Maxxed Gold Estimator privacy policy.

Important limitation

Color and surface appearance cannot identify chemistry. Results are not an assay or a valuation.
