Capabilities

What Gold Estimator does

An offline visual material estimator with guided multi-angle capture, reference scale, mask correction, material review, conservative ranges, saved batches, and CSV export.

01

Capture multiple angles

Collect top, side, rear, and oblique views with coverage and image-quality checks.

02

Review the analysis

Adjust mask sensitivity and confirm or change visible-material groupings before trusting the output.

03

Read conservative ranges

Review visible share, density assumptions, volume, weight, and confidence ranges with clear limitations.

04

Compare and export

Save local batches, compare results, copy a summary, or choose a CSV destination.

Availability

The visual-estimate workflow is implemented and is moving through automated checks, signed build work, physical fixture testing, and final disclosures.

Privacy

Captures remain in app-private storage and leave only when the user chooses an export or sharing destination. Read the detailed privacy policy.

Important limitation

Color and surface appearance cannot identify chemistry. Results are not an assay, appraisal, or valuation.
