Maxxed Remote Privacy Policy

Maxxed Remote controls compatible televisions over the user's local network. The current documented release does not create a Maxxed account, collect analytics, transmit personal data to Maxxed Technical Systems, sell personal data, or include an active advertising SDK. The app is designed so saved television details and pairing credentials stay on the user's device unless the user separately sends support information by email.

Data processed

  • Television names, manufacturer labels, model labels where available, or user-assigned nicknames
  • Local television IP addresses, local-network discovery responses, and device identifiers needed to reconnect
  • Pairing credentials, authorization tokens, secure-session material, and certificate fingerprints used to control a paired television
  • Local network state, Wi-Fi state, and connectivity information needed to discover and control compatible televisions
  • User settings such as preferred TV, saved shortcuts, keypad state, and connection preferences
  • Support details only if the user chooses to email them, such as device model, app version, TV model, and troubleshooting notes

Permissions and purpose

  • Internet: Communicate with compatible televisions and supported TV control interfaces over the user's local or private network.
  • Network and Wi-Fi state: Confirm network availability, detect connection changes, and support local discovery.
  • Wi-Fi multicast: Receive SSDP or similar local discovery responses from compatible televisions.
  • Nearby Wi-Fi devices: Support local Wi-Fi discovery on Android versions that require this permission for nearby network devices.

Storage and security

Saved televisions, pairing credentials, trust fingerprints, shortcuts, and connection preferences remain in Android app-private storage. Pairing secrets are protected with Android Keystore-backed encryption where supported. The app does not maintain a developer-operated cloud copy of saved televisions.

Collection and sharing

The app does not send saved television information, pairing credentials, local IP addresses, or viewing behavior to Maxxed Technical Systems, advertising networks, analytics providers, or data brokers. Information leaves the app only when the user deliberately sends support details, uses Android sharing, or communicates directly with the television on the local network.

Retention

Saved television records and settings remain until the user removes a television, clears app data, or uninstalls the app. Support emails are retained only as long as reasonably needed for support, abuse prevention, operational records, or legal obligations.

Deletion

Delete individual saved televisions inside the app. Clearing Android app storage or uninstalling removes the remaining local data. To request deletion of support emails or beta/support records sent to Maxxed Technical Systems, email privacy@techmaxxed.com from the address used for the request.

Third-party services

Television manufacturers receive commands over the user's local network according to the television's own software, pairing flow, firmware, and privacy practices. Google Play may process installation, purchase, crash, security, and device diagnostics under Google's policies. Maxxed Remote does not proxy TV commands through a developer server.

Children

Maxxed Remote is a general utility and is not directed to children. It is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

Questions may be sent to privacy@techmaxxed.com.
