What Remote does

A streamlined Android remote for compatible Samsung Tizen and LG webOS televisions, built around secure local-network control and fast everyday access.

01

Find compatible TVs

Discover supported Samsung Tizen and LG webOS televisions on your local network.

02

Pair with confidence

Approval and pairing credentials stay protected through Android Keystore encryption.

03

Keep control close

Use navigation, volume, channels, keypad input, saved TVs, and supported app shortcuts.

04

Designed for local use

Remote commands stay on the private network and only private IP addresses can be added.

Product truth

Status, privacy, and limits

Clear expectations are part of the product, not an afterthought.

Availability

The signed Android build is completing television compatibility checks and Play Store release preparation. Pre-release testing requests are welcome even before a public store launch.

Privacy

Pairing credentials are encrypted on the device. Remote-control traffic remains on the local network. Read the detailed Maxxed Remote privacy policy.

Important limitation

Feature and streaming-app support varies by television model, region, and manufacturer.
