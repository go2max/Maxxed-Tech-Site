Capabilities

What Remote does

A focused Android TV remote for compatible Samsung Tizen and LG webOS televisions, with local-network control, saved TVs, keypad input, and app shortcuts.

01

Find compatible TVs

Discover supported Samsung Tizen and LG webOS televisions on the same private network.

02

Pair with confidence

TV approval and pairing credentials stay protected through Android app-private storage and Keystore support where available.

03

Use the controls that matter

Navigate, adjust volume, change channels, enter keypad text, reopen saved TVs, and launch supported shortcuts.

04

Keep commands local

Remote commands are designed for the private network instead of routing TV control through a Maxxed server.

Availability

The signed Android build is in release preparation while television compatibility and store materials are verified.

Privacy

Pairing credentials are stored on the device. Remote-control traffic is designed to stay on the local network. Read the detailed privacy policy.

Important limitation

Feature support varies by television model, firmware, region, and manufacturer control interface.
