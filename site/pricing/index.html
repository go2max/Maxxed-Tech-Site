Do you collect card numbers on TechMaxxed.com?

No. Checkout buttons route to hosted payment pages or a manual invoice request. Card details are handled by the payment processor.

What happens after purchase?

Fulfillment depends on the product. Android products may use Google Play or tester access, while WordPress tools receive download and setup instructions.

Can I buy before a public launch?

Only when the product page and checkout flow clearly state the current access level. Development-stage access may be fulfilled as early access or manual setup.

Can I request a refund?

Email support with the product, purchase email, order reference, and issue. Refund handling depends on the store or payment processor used.
