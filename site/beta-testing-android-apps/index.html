What customers usually ask

Use these notes to choose the right product or send a cleaner support request.

Can I request a product that is not public yet?

Yes. That is exactly what pre-release testing is for when the tester and product are a good fit.

Is beta testing paid?

No. Public pages describe testing as voluntary and unpaid, with optional public credit when approved by the tester.

How do I apply?

Use the beta form and include your Google Account, device, Android version, selected apps, and useful testing notes.
